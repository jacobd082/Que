Security & Privacy

At Que, we value you Privacy.

When you communicate with Que, your data is fully encrypted. Your ISP cannot read any traffic from you to Que, and vice-versa.

Our providers all use HTTPS, that encrypts and secures all data.

Que does not store any data regarding you, your messages, your device, any identifying information (e.g. your IP address), etc.

If you have any concerns let us know.
